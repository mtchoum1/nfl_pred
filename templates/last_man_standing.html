Game Rules

  • Progression: If your chosen team wins, you advance to the next week with your perfect record intact.
  • Imperfect Record: If your chosen team loses or the game ends in a tie, your record becomes "imperfect." You are not eliminated and can continue to make picks each week.
  • No Repeats: You cannot select the same team more than once during the season.
  • Divisional Lockout: Once you pick from a division, it is locked until you have picked a team from all 8 divisions. After that, the cycle resets.
  • Sequential Picks: You must make your picks in order. You cannot pick for a future week until you have made a pick for the current one.
  • Weekly Lockout: Picks for a given week become locked when the first game of that week begins. You cannot make or change a pick after this time.
  • No Submission: If you forget to make a pick for a week, it counts as a loss, and your record will become imperfect.
  • Winner: The player with the most total wins at the end of the season is the winner. The current streak is used as a tie-breaker.
